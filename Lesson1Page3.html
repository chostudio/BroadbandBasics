Lesson 1: What is Broadband?

  • Broadband provides access to services that require a large amount of data transmission. An example is video conferencing for work, education, and telehealth.
  • Broadband is starting to become a necessity, as more and more services like government forms/documents, homework, commerce, etc. become online only.
